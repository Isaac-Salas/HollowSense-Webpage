Don't loose yourself.

Marooned on an hostile planet, a pilot tries to unravel his past before madness dwells him…or worse, before he dies to be cloned and repeat this nightmare once again.

Alexandro, a colonizer cloned for the umpteenth time, and Roxana, an AI bonded to him, seek to terraform Reba, a hostile planet ruled by parasites and other wandering clones. The mysteries of Alex's past unravel as he descends into the depths of Reba; however, as he interacts with each of these agents, he will question whether he should escape the planet or save it before being consumed by madness.
