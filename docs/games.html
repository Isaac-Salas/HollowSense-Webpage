Story Sumary

Looking for a new earth the program NEO-GAIA create clones to help in the investigation and conquering of a new planet. After many years of looking, tribulation Lucry Gokstad found REBA. Earth created a diplomatic approach with the people of REBA. This people accepted the Earthlings with open arms, teaching them their culture and tradition. Not knowing that all this knowledge would be used against them. What was supposed to be a gentle and sympathetic conection turn into a genocide when the NEO-GAIA ejecutives decided this was their new earth. Clones were send to kill and terraform REBA to turn into EARTH BETA. After many years of war. Earth decide to send their ultimate weapon: Teratogens, a biological weapon created with the only objective to destroy the Rebarians. The introduction of this weapon brought the end of a war. Earth could feel their victory. Rebarians feeling close their defeat, they biological alter the Teratogens to destroy the clones that betrayed them. Teratogens went from just destroying Rebarians to a necessity to eat human's flesh. Rebarians were destroyed but the only rulers of Reba became the Teratogens, making impossible for Reba to become the new earth. REBA was left forgotten but the clones does not know that.

Features


  • Tense First-Person Survival: Every step could be your last. Conserve your resources, make every shot count, and navigate a hostile environment where danger lurks in every shadow.

  • Strategic Battery Management: Your life depends on energy — literally. Collect and distribute power across your battery modules to keep crucial systems online. Choose wisely; running out of energy might mean sealing your fate.

  • Immersive, Oppressive Atmosphere: Dive into a world drenched in tension and mystery. The environment reacts subtly to your presence, building an eerie sense of unease as you unravel its dark secrets.

  • Resourceful Exploration: Survival isn't just about shooting — it's about managing what little you have. Scour every corner for precious energy, hidden tools, and clues that could mean the difference between escape and oblivion.

  • Unravel a Cryptic Narrative: The story won't reveal itself easily. Piece together fragmented clues scattered across a desolate world and uncover the truth behind the shadows.

  • A World of Unfettered Imagination: Death Pilot draws from the essence of classic survival horror while crafting a wholly original universe. Every decision, every moment, shapes your fate.
